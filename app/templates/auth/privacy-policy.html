Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure Storage: Personal data and API tokens are encrypted at rest in our databases
  • Access Controls: Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only and logged for audit purposes
  • Secure APIs: We use OAuth 2.0 for third-party service authentication and never store your passwords
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security reviews of our systems and practices
  • Data Isolation: User data is logically isolated to prevent unauthorized cross-user access

While we implement these security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your data using industry best practices.

YouTube API Services and Google User Data

Creatrics uses YouTube API Services to provide video upload, optimization, and analytics features. When you connect your YouTube account to Creatrics:

  • Data We Access: We access your YouTube channel information, video metadata (titles, descriptions, tags, thumbnails), video list, captions, comments, and analytics data (views, watch time, engagement metrics).
  • How We Use YouTube Data: We use this data solely to provide you with our video optimization, upload, and analytics services. We do not use your YouTube data for any other purpose.
  • Data Storage: We store minimal YouTube data necessary to provide our services (e.g., video IDs, titles, thumbnails for your dashboard). We do not store video files or detailed analytics data permanently.
  • Data Sharing: We do not share your YouTube data with any third parties except as required to provide our services or as required by law.
  • Data Retention: We retain YouTube data only as long as necessary to provide our services. When you disconnect your YouTube account or delete your Creatrics account, we delete all associated YouTube data.
  • Revoking Access: You can revoke our access to your YouTube account at any time through your Google Account permissions page or by disconnecting YouTube in your Creatrics account settings.

Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. This means we will only use your YouTube data to provide and improve user-facing features that are prominent in our application's user interface.

TikTok Services and User Data

TikTok Official API (Content Posting)

Creatrics uses TikTok's official Content Posting API to provide video upload and content posting features. When you connect your TikTok account to Creatrics:

  • Data We Access: We access your TikTok profile information (display name, avatar, open_id) and video posting capabilities.
  • How We Use TikTok Data: We use your TikTok connection solely to upload videos and post content to your TikTok account on your behalf. We generate AI-powered titles and hashtags that you can use when posting to TikTok.
  • Data Storage: We store your encrypted TikTok access tokens securely in our database. We do not store your TikTok password or video content.
  • Data Sharing: We do not share your TikTok data with any third parties except as required to provide our video upload service or as required by law.
  • Data Retention: We retain your TikTok access tokens only while your account is connected. When you disconnect your TikTok account or delete your Creatrics account, we immediately delete all associated TikTok tokens and data.
  • Revoking Access: You can revoke our access to your TikTok account at any time by disconnecting TikTok in your Creatrics account settings or through TikTok's app authorization settings.

We comply with TikTok's API Terms of Service and Developer Policy. We only request the minimum permissions necessary to provide our services (user.info.basic, video.upload, video.publish).

Third-Party Social Media Data Services (TikTok & X/Twitter)

For features such as competitor analysis, trend research, and keyword discovery, we use third-party data providers (including RapidAPI services) that collect publicly available social media data from platforms like TikTok and X (Twitter). This data collection:

  • Data Collected: Public profile information (usernames, display names, follower counts), public post/video metadata (titles, view counts, engagement metrics), and search results.
  • Data Source: This data is collected from publicly accessible content via third-party aggregation services, not directly from official platform APIs.
  • Purpose: Used solely to provide you with competitive insights, trend analysis, and content research tools.
  • Limitations: We only access publicly available data and do not access private accounts or restricted content.
  • User Control: If you are a content creator on TikTok or X and want your public data excluded from our research tools, please visit creatrics.com/tiktok-data-optout or contact us at support@creatrics.com.

Important: The third-party social media data collection is separate from any connected accounts (TikTok OAuth, X manual entry). We do not connect or correlate data from third-party sources with your personal account credentials.
